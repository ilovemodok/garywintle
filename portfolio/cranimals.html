Cranimals: Designing Charlie

The Client

Cranimals is an American dogfood distributor that specializes in healthy, vegan dog treats.

The Challenge

I was contacted by Cranimals to come up with a new character, codename “Charlie” and package design for new line of “Zendog” dog treats.

My Role

  • Character Design
  • Logo Design
  • Package Design

My Tools

  • Hand Drawing
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Initial Sketches

I went to work right with zen thoughts in mind. I wanted to come up with something that mixed cute doggy character packaging we often see in the aisles with something a little serene and worthy of the ZenDog title

Narrowing down and finetuning from there

After some chats with the client over the sketches I’d sent them, we decided on which parts of which sketches were what they wanted in their final character design. They also decided that Charlie should be sitting on a giant pumpkin surrounded by cranberries since those were both key ingredients to their vegan dog food. I was happy to customize the art to their liking. It’s really a fun thing to see a client view something they helped to create for the first time.

Finalizing the

With the final pencils approved, I went to work on vectoring and coloring the art in Adobe Illustrator. It was a bit of a challenge coming up with colors, but I decided to use the big pumpkin Charlie was now sitting on as inspiration! I was able to play off the oranges not only to design Charlie (with browns that have tints of orange), but also to design the whole package.

Pack it in a doggy bag

Upon final approval, the packaging was set up and printed. The results were great, with Cranimals winning Pet Product New Editor’s Choice 2010 Seal of Approval, even cheekily adding that the ‘animated’ cover model Charlie was a big part of their success. This was a fun team to work with. It also allowed me to use my illustration along with my graphic design skills to come up with a fun concept that had great results. A win for everyone, especially vegan dogs!
